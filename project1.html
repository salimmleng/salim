ImmunoHub: Vaccine Management System

ImmunoHub is a full-featured vaccine management platform that streamlines the entire vaccination process. It enables users to securely book vaccines, manage dose scheduling, and provide feedback. Featuring a dual-access dashboard, it provides personalized experiences for users and admins. The admin panel allows efficient management of vaccine requests, dose schedules, and user feedback.
